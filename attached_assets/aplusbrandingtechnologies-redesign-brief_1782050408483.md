# A Plus Branding Technologies --- Website Redesign Brief

## Objective

Transform the website into a premium, modern, conversion-focused AI
automation and software agency website targeted at SMEs.

Primary business positioning:

> AI Automation & Custom Software for SMEs

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Design Direction

## Brand Feel

-   Premium
-   Modern
-   Trustworthy
-   Technical but approachable
-   Minimalist with strong visual hierarchy

## Visual References

Use inspiration from: - Clay - Ramotion - Linear - Vercel - Supabase -
Framer

Avoid: - Generic corporate templates - Overuse of gradients - Excessive
animations - Dense text blocks
