What is an AI receptionist — and how it books jobs while you sleep

Every missed call is a customer who phoned the next business on the list. An AI receptionist makes sure that never happens again.

An AI receptionist is a smart assistant that lives on your website and chat channels. It greets every visitor instantly, answers their questions, captures their details and books the job — automatically, 24 hours a day. At A-Plus, ours is called Sam.

Why it matters for a local business

Most enquiries come in when you're busy on a job, driving, or asleep. If nobody answers within a few minutes, the customer moves on. An AI receptionist closes that gap so no lead slips away.

What it actually does

  • Answers instantly, 24/7 — no "we'll get back to you", no missed messages.
  • Qualifies the lead — asks the right questions and captures name, number and what they need.
  • Books the job — offers times or hands off cleanly to WhatsApp so you finish the conversation.
  • Never has an off day — public holidays, 2am, month-end — it's always on.
Most web designers can build you a page. Few can give you a 24/7 receptionist built into it.

Won't it feel robotic?

Done well, no. A good AI receptionist is friendly, on-brand and knows your services, prices and area. When a conversation needs a human, it hands straight over to you on WhatsApp — so customers always feel looked after.

The maths is simple

If your average job is worth a few hundred rand and an AI receptionist saves you just one or two missed enquiries a month, it has already paid for itself. Everything after that is extra income you were previously losing.
